Eugene Delta - Black Dogs EP

“BLACK DOGS” is an inner dialogue about goodbyes and reunions, icy nights in between and sleepless winters thereafter. Also friendships and the sun.

“BLACK DOGS” contains 4 tracks ranging from dreamy folk-pop down into dark electro chasms flooded with distorted choirs and up into airy skies filled with sparkling beats and jubilant strings. Enjoy!
